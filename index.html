Project AMV Overload

Welcome to Project AMV Overload, a unique and dynamic fusion of anime music videos and DJ sets.


Our goal is to bring the excitement of both anime and electronic music to fans in a whole new way. Imagine watching your favorite anime scenes set to the beat of the hottest tracks, all while being transported to an immersive and electrifying world of sound and color.


But it's not just about what we bring to the table - we want to involve the community in the excitement! That's why we welcome fans to submit their own anime music videos to be considered for use in our events. Join us for an unforgettable experience unlike any other and get ready to be overloaded with pure anime-fueled excitement!

Contribute to Project AMV Overload

We're always looking for anime and electronic music fans to join us and contribute to the Project AMV Overload community. If you're interested in submitting your own AMV content, here's what you need to know:

  1. Create: To create a AMV you must follow these steps:

    • Find a music track. If you have a particular song in mind, great! Just clear the song with us first! Or check the list

    • Edit your AMV! Make sure the music is placed right at the begining in the movie editing software

  2. Requirements: Your AMV must meet the following requirements:

    • The AMV must be created by you

    • The AMV must be of high quality, suitable for use in a live performance

    • The AMV must be in 1080p mp4 format

    • Name the AMV in the format "AMV - [Name_of_the_musicfile].mp4".
      E.g AMV - David Guetta & Bebe Rexha - I'm Good (Crankdat Remix).mp4

  3. How to Submit: To submit your AMV, simply email us at ProjectAmvOverload@gmail.com with the following information:

    • A download link to your AMV

    • Your artist name/social media

  4. Review Process: Our team will review all submissions and respond within 3 days. If your AMV is selected, it will be used in live shows and highlights will be featured on social media channels.

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Project AMV Overload. We're looking forward to seeing what you can bring to the project!

How it works

Project AMV Overload is designed to bring together anime and electronic music in a seamless, synchronized experience. To make this possible, we've developed a special software that runs on a computer and is connected to a DJ mixer. Additionally, we have created a server software that runs on the DJ's computer and relays information from the mixer to the video playback software.


Playing music and video in real-time during a live DJ performance is a complex task that requires the ability to bring in and combine music on the fly. The tracks also need to be played in the same tempo, or beats per minute (BPM). Normally on big stages, there is a time delay for the video, which is undesired and needs to be compensated for. Our software takes care of all of these challenges, allowing the DJ to focus on what they do best: creating a dynamic and engaging performance.


The video playback software receives information from the DJ mixer about the current song name and elapsed time, and then it loads and plays the right AMV for the song in real-time, ensuring that the video and audio are perfectly synchronized. All of the AMV videos are stored on the computer, ready to be played at the touch of a button.


With our software, the DJ has full control over the visual aspect of their performance, allowing them to create a truly immersive and unforgettable experience for their audience.
